# VCO 1

Here is my first VCO based on the work of Moritz Klein and his great series of VCO videos found [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QBatvo8bCa4&list=PLHeL0JWdJLvTuGCyC3qvx0RM39YvopVQN). I based the schematic on his work and the Erika Synths kit schematic but I added in the sawtooth-to-triangle wave converter. 

The PCB design is my own and I designed it as a two layer pcb made of two double sided pcbs. I ended up self-etching, hence the registration pin-holes next to the drill holes for the standoffs on the pcb designs.

I hope you find this useful, and a huge thanks to Moritz Klein for getting me started down this path!
